# SafeScoring — Patent Policy & Defensive Patent Pledge

**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

---

## 1. Defensive Patent Pledge

SafeScoring LLC hereby pledges that it will not use any of its patents, patent applications, or patent-related intellectual property offensively against any party, except as a counterclaim or defense in response to a patent infringement action first brought against SafeScoring LLC, its users, or its contributors.

This pledge is **irrevocable** and applies to all current and future patents owned by SafeScoring LLC, including but not limited to those listed in Section 3 below.

### 1.1 Scope of Pledge

This pledge covers:

- All patents and patent applications filed by SafeScoring LLC
- All patents acquired by SafeScoring LLC through assignment or purchase
- All continuations, divisionals, and reissues of the above

### 1.2 Binding on Successors

This pledge is binding on SafeScoring LLC and any successor entity, assignee, or acquirer of SafeScoring's patent portfolio. Any transfer, sale, or assignment of SafeScoring patents must include this defensive pledge as a condition of transfer.

### 1.3 Revocation Conditions

This pledge may only be revoked with respect to a specific party if that party:

(a) Initiates a patent infringement action against SafeScoring LLC, its users, or its contributors; or
(b) Acquires patents for the purpose of asserting them offensively against SafeScoring LLC, its users, or its contributors.

## 2. Additional Patent Grant

Subject to the terms and conditions of this grant, SafeScoring LLC hereby grants to every person or entity who receives, uses, modifies, or distributes SafeScoring software (as licensed under the AGPL-3.0 or a commercial license) a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable patent license to make, have made, use, offer to sell, sell, import, and otherwise transfer the Work, where such license applies only to those patent claims licensable by SafeScoring LLC that are necessarily infringed by SafeScoring's contribution(s) alone or by combination of SafeScoring's contribution(s) with the Work to which such contribution(s) was submitted.

### 2.1 Termination

This patent grant terminates automatically with respect to any party that:

(a) Files a patent infringement lawsuit (including a cross-claim or counterclaim) alleging that SafeScoring software or any contribution incorporated within SafeScoring software constitutes direct or contributory patent infringement; or
(b) Assists, encourages, or funds any third party in filing such a lawsuit.

Upon termination, the terminated party loses all rights granted under this patent grant, effective as of the date the lawsuit was filed.

### 2.2 Scope

This patent grant is supplemental to (and does not replace) the implicit patent license included in the AGPL-3.0 license. It provides additional explicit patent protection to all users and contributors of SafeScoring software.

## 5. Third-Party Patent Notice

SafeScoring respects the valid patent rights of others. If you believe that SafeScoring software infringes a valid patent, please contact us at patents@safescoring.io with:

1. Identification of the patent(s) at issue (number, title, relevant claims)
2. Identification of the specific SafeScoring functionality alleged to infringe
3. A detailed claim chart mapping patent claims to SafeScoring features

SafeScoring will review all legitimate patent notices in good faith and respond within thirty (30) business days.
